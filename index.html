Me, Myself, and I

Full Stack Developer Interested in Building Well Designed Products That Matter.

I am Software Engineer, Massachusetts native, and in my free time somewhat of an artist. I graduated McGill University in Software Engineering with a Music Science minor. I believe that well designed technology is the most powerful tool for imparting social good and desire to continue building products that help us express ourselves and connect with others. When I am not landlocked and developing I am surfing and dreaming. Currently in Montreal, although that is subject to change.
